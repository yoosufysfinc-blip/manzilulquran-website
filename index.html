About the Academy

Where tradition meets accessibility.

ManzilulQuran Academy was founded with a singular intent — to make authentic Quranic and Arabic education available to every student, regardless of geography or background.

Our methodology combines classical pedagogy with modern online tools, allowing learners to study with qualified scholars from the comfort of their homes — through individual or batch classes.

Our Programs

Seven paths to mastery.

Each course is structured by qualified scholars and delivered with the rigor of traditional Islamic learning — adapted for the modern student.

Quran Memorization

Online Madrasa
